How to Print to PDF or Paper Open any planner sheet, and follow these setup recommendations in browser print popup (Ctrl+P):
  • Destination: Save to PDF or select your Color Printer
  • Layout: Portrait
  • Paper Size: Letter (8.5" x 11") or A4
  • Margins: None (or Default)
  • Options: Check the checkbox for "Background graphics" (crucial for theme colors)
