🖨️ How to Print to PDF or Paper
Open any planner page and press Ctrl+P (Cmd+P) with these parameters:
  • Orientation: Portrait
  • Paper Size: Letter (8.5" x 11") or A4
  • Margins: None (or Default)
  • Background Graphics: Must be Checked (enables theme background graphics)
