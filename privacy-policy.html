Privacy Policy

Buddy Planner — by MarketingSoulPro

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

MarketingSoulPro (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the Buddy Planner application (the “Software”). This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected when you use our Software.

By using the Buddy Planner, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Software.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Planner Data (Stored Locally)

All planner data you enter—including text, checkboxes, habit logs, expense entries, drawings, theme preferences, and settings—is stored exclusively in your browser’s localStorage.

  • This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, cloud, or third party.
  • Clearing your browser data (cache, cookies, site data) will erase this information. We strongly recommend using the built-in Export JSON feature in the Backup & Sync panel to create a safety copy.
  • We cannot access, recover, or restore your planner data because it never reaches our servers.

2.2 License Activation Data (Sent to Server)

When you activate your license key, the following information is sent to our license server for the sole purpose of validating your purchase and registering your device:

  • Your license key
  • Your name (as entered during activation)
  • Your email address
  • Your chosen device name (e.g. “Laptop”)

This information is only used for license management, including verifying your entitlement, limiting activations to 3 devices, and providing customer support.

2.3 Automated Data (Not Collected)

The Buddy Planner does not use cookies, analytics trackers, fingerprinting, or any automated data collection tools. We do not collect usage statistics, page views, click data, or any behavioural information.

3. How We Use Your Data

The limited data we receive (name, email, device name, license key during activation) is used exclusively for:

  • Validating your license key and granting access
  • Registering and managing your 3-device activation limit
  • Providing customer support and license recovery
  • Communicating with you about your license or account if you initiate contact

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party.

Exceptions (where required by law):

  • If compelled by a valid legal request (court order, subpoena), we may disclose information as required by law.
  • If necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights of others.
Third-party services: The Software may load fonts from Google Fonts. Google’s use of data is governed by their own privacy policy. We do not control and are not responsible for third-party data practices.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information you provide during license activation. Our license server uses industry-standard security measures. However:

  • No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
  • Your planner data’s security depends on your own browser and device security. Keep your device and browser updated.
  • We cannot be held responsible for unauthorised access to your device or browser storage.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (limited to license activation data).
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your license activation data. Note that this will revoke your license.
  • Data portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: buddyplannerpk@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

License activation data (name, email, device name, license key) is retained for as long as your license is active. If you request deletion of this data, your license will be deactivated.

Planner data is stored in your browser and is not retained on any server. It exists only as long as you keep it in your browser or in exported files you create.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Buddy Planner is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of the Software after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
