My Dopamine Menu

Healthy rewards & stimulation quick-lists
WEEK
SELECTED:
🍇 Appetizers (5-Min Starters)
Quick activities to build momentum and overcome task paralysis.
Drink a glass of cold water
Do 10 jumping jacks or stretch for 2 minutes
Listen to one favorite upbeat song
Tidy your workspace for 5 minutes
Write down your next tiny task
🥪 Sides (15-Min Energy boosts)
Short rewards and resets to recharge without losing focus.
Take a walk outside or around the house
Enjoy a cup of coffee, tea, or a favorite snack mindfully
Watch one funny video or short comedy clip
Practice deep breathing or a guided meditation
Call or text a supportive friend or family member
🍲 Mains (30-Min Focused rewards)
Meaningful activities that provide satisfaction after progress.
Play a favorite game for 30 minutes
Read a chapter of a book you enjoy
Work on a hobby (drawing, crafts, music)
Watch one episode of a favorite TV show
Spend quality time with a pet or loved one
🍰 Desserts (Large Milestones)
Special rewards reserved for completing major goals.
Order your favorite meal or dessert
Buy a small item from your wishlist
Plan a movie night or self-care evening
Take a day trip to a favorite place
Enjoy a guilt-free "do nothing" relaxation session
🚨 Emergency Dopamine Boosters
Use when feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or unable to start.
5-Minute Rule: Commit to working for just 5 minutes
Body Double: Work alongside a friend, colleague, or virtual focus session
Change Your Environment: Move to a different room or workspace
Brain Dump: Write down everything on your mind without organizing it
Sensory Reset: Use fidget tools, chew gum, stretch, or splash cold water on your face
